Sires & Dams

We are very pleased with our breeding program and endeavor to only bring in animals with even temperment and good dispositions. Our dogs are not just part of our program but are part of our family. We take great joy in working with our animals and are very selective of the new stock that we bring into our program. Our sincere desire is to bring that same joy that we get from our Aussies into your homes!

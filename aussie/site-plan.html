American Aussies Site Plan

Christopher Ditto

WDD 13.10

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to gain a bigger market share by advertising the sale of Australian Shepard puppies. The site will show the breeder's sires, dams, and available puppies along with information on past litters. The site will also include information about the breed, the breeder, and AKC specifications.

Audience

The audience for the website would be anyone interested in purchasing an Australian Shepard or someone who would like to know a bit more about the breed.
